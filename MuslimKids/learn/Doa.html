Dhuha Prayer Du'a

Dhuha Doa often being read in the morning in Dhuha time. Most of the time Dhuha Prayer is for someone seeking rizq. So if someone has no time to perform Dhuha Prayer they also can read the Dhuha Du'a as a routine every morning

Parents Du'a

This doa is specify for our parents that had took care of us. Mother the one who giving birth of us, the one who taking care of us in her womb in 9 months.

Father, without the meaning of tired still searching for nafkah for his family. Giving us comfortable place to stay, give good education to us, give us a healthy foog and drink as he want us to grow to become someone useful in the future

So, this doa is dedicate for their hardwork as they have make us a useful human.

Wearing Clothes Du'a

People nowadays always forgot to recite du'a when they are wearing their clothes. Eventhough reciting this du'a is not compulsary but it is a good practice to recite this du'a before wearing clothes.

Before Study Du'a

The du'a before study is a must because we need barakah of the ilm that had been given from our god, Allah.

When we recite this du'a, Insyaa Allah our god will give us tranquil when we are seeking for His knowledge.
