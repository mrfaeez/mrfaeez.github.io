Solat Fardhu

Solat Fardhu is the solat that must for a muslim to perform it. It have 5 times per day in which are Subh, Zuhr, Asr, Maghrib and Ishak.

The one who not performing the Solat Fardhu will be asked when there are in Barzakh, and who did not perform it will get punishment.

For Subh, it has 2 rakaat, for Zuhr, Asr and Isyak they have 4 rakaat per day. Meanwhile for Maghrib prayer is performed for 3 rakaat only.

Solat Sunat

Rawatib Prayer consist of 2 which are

      Muakkad(Rasulullah perform often)
      Ghair Muakkad(Rasulullah do perfrom the prayer, but sometime he did not)

    From Sahih Muslim:

    مَا مِنْ عَبْدٍ مُسْلِمٍ يُصَلِّي لِلَّهِ كُلَّ يَوْمٍ ثِنْتَيْ عَشْرَةَ رَكْعَةً تَطَوُّعًا غَيْرَ فَرِيضَةٍ إِلاَّ بَنَى اللَّهُ لَهُ بَيْتًا فِي الْجَنَّةِ

    Meaning:A muslim servant pray because of Allah SWT 12 rakaat per day as tathawwu' and not fardh, and Allah will develop for him a house in heaven.


Muakkad Prayer

      2 rakaat before Subh
      2 rakaat before Zuhr
      2 rakaat After Maghrib
      2 rakaat after Isya'

Ghair Muakkad Prayer

      The last 2 rakaat before Zuhr
      The last 2 rakaat after Zuhr
      4 rakaat before Asr
      2 rakaat before Maghrib
      2 rakaat before Isya'
